Porepunkah

The imagery from 1983 was taken only weeks before the Ash Wednesday fires that claimed the lives of 47 people in February 1983. At this time the main industry in Porepunkah was timber harvesting, the mill being visible in the top left corner of the 1983 image. Note that the big roundabout is currently being built and the Ovens River bridge does not yet exist, the old bridge is still in use.
